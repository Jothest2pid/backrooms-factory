THE BACKROOMS

a non-euclidean top-down walking sim

  • WASD / arrows — walk   Shift — run
  • right-click — mine / disassemble / strip carpet / deconstruct
  • left-click — inspect / use machine   C — crafting
  • 1–9 — hotbar   left-click — use held (place / shoot / throw / eat)
  • C — craft/tinker   I — inventory & equip   V — blink
  • B — build   R — rotate   ~ — god   P — debug   L — export log

Rooms are stitched together by doorways — not by space. Walk a loop and the geometry won't add up. Connected rooms hang around you at half opacity; the one you're standing in snaps to full focus.

EQUIPMENT — click a slot to unequip
INVENTORY — click a trinket to equip · I to close
CRAFTING — C to close
HOW TO PROGRESS — click GUIDE or press H to close

1. Scavenge. Right-click furniture to break it for wood, fabric, scrap and fuel. It sometimes also drops loot or a trinket. Right-click carpet to strip it, and ore patches to mine them.

2. First tools. Press C to craft. Make planks, then a wooden pickaxe and an axe, then a workbench. Select it in the hotbar (keys 1-9) and left-click an open tile to place it.

3. Parts. Standing near the workbench unlocks more recipes: wooden gears, electrum plate, wire, and a machine frame. Mine electrum and stygium veins for metal.

4. Automate. Lay a belt, then drop a smelter onto the belt. Ore riding past it becomes ingots. Add a presser further along to turn ingots into plates. Chain more processors to keep up with a fast belt.

5. Power. Build a wood generator and load fuel into it (left-click with fuel). Powered machines draw from the grid. If you overbuild, the PWR meter browns out and machines slow down.

6. Gear up. Press I to open inventory and equipment. Click a trinket to equip it into one of the 10 slots. Build a tinker bench to fuse duplicate trinkets into stronger ones, or merge different ones into capstones like boots, wings, or the Phone.

7. Survive the dark. Dark rooms spawn enemies. Craft a musket, a bow, or an auto-turret. Make an alchemy cauldron and turn brimstone + powder into black sulfur for ammo and grenades. Eat food and set a bedroll so death sends you back to it instead of spawn.

8. Go deeper. Build the crusher, assembler, crucible, loom and animal pen. Find schematics in library rooms to unlock alternate recipes. Floor and wallpaper your base to make it yours.
